Hackathon @ EvoHax!!

4/20/2015

This past weekend members from West Chester University's Computer Science Club and Women in Computer Science Club participated in EvoHax. Our goal: 24 hours to create something amazing, something awesome all relating to web accessability. Our team decided to create a chrome extension that would allow visually-impaired people to easily traverse a web page. Check it out!

Super Science Saturday 2014!

11/2/2014

Today West Chester University opened its' doors to Super Science Saturday!! An afternoon filled with fun STEM related activities for young girls intrested in science! The Women in Computer Science Club did a programming tutorial with Alice, a 3D programming environment which makes it super easy to create animations for telling stories, playing games, or even a videos that can be shared on the internet. The best part is it's free to use! Using drag and drop code Alice is perfect for the young girl interested in programming.
